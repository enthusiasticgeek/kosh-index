## 1. Summary

- Techniques implemented in the kosh-index tooling and governance infrastructure
  are **publicly disclosed** from their first git commit dates, constituting
  prior art under 35 U.S.C. §102.
- Contributors **cannot** patent anything derived from this registry's code,
  governance model, or tooling.
- Their employers **cannot** either — [`CLA.md`](CLA.md) §4a requires
  contributors to covenant non-assertion on behalf of themselves, their
  employer, successors, and assigns.
- If anyone asserts a patent against this registry or its users, **all their
  licenses and contributor status terminate automatically** (CLA §4b).
- The Operator holds **no offensive patents**. Any patents obtained in the
  future will be used **only defensively**.

## 2. Patent Non-Assertion Pledge

The Operator hereby makes the following irrevocable public pledge:

> **Neither the Operator nor the Registry will assert any patent covering
> inventions implemented in this software against any person or entity for
> their use, study, modification, or distribution of this software or software
> substantially interoperable with it, except in defensive response to a patent
> assertion initiated by that person or entity against this Registry or its
> users.**

This pledge is binding on the Operator and on any successor or assign of the
Registry.

## 3. What the CLA Requires of Contributors

Under [`CLA.md`](CLA.md) §4 and §4a, every Approved Contributor:

1. **Grants a patent license** covering patent claims necessarily infringed by
   their Contribution alone or in combination with the Registry.
2. **Covenants not to assert** any patent covering innovations derived from or
   substantially similar to their Contribution or implemented in the Registry
   at the time of their Contribution.
3. **Cannot file or fund patent applications** on such innovations.
4. **Triggers automatic termination** of all licenses and contributor status if
   they or their employer assert a patent against the Registry or its users.

## 4. Reporting Patent Threats

If you become aware of:

- A patent or patent application that may read on this registry's tooling or
  governance approach
- A patent assertion or licensing demand directed at this registry or its users
- A contributor or employer who has violated the non-assertion covenant in the CLA

Please report it **immediately** by opening a **private** GitHub Security
Advisory (preferred) or by emailing the Operator at
&lt;enthusiasticgeek@gmail.com&gt; with subject line `[PATENT]`.
